Q.) Explain Joins and type of joins with suitable examples 
A.) In DBMS, a join operation is used to combine rows from two or more tables via a related column, Joins helps retrieve meaningful data by establishing a connection between tables. 
Types of DBMS joins:
	1. INNER JOIN
	2. OUTER JOIN
	3. LEFT JOIN
	4. RIGHT JOIN
	5. FULL JOIN
	6. CROSS JOIN
	7. SELF JOIN
	8. NATURAL JOIN
